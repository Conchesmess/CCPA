OTData

OTData is the website of the Oakland Technical High School Computer Academy community. The site is used by teachers, students, alumni and parents to communicate and to find critical information like the Okalnd Tech Course Catalog. From OTData teachers and staff are able to send email and text messages to student and parents. OTData is also connected to Google Classroom so teachers are able to access lists of all their students for purposes of communication and attendence. Students can access OTData to check-in for distance learning. In the neat future parents and alumni will be able to access the site to update their contact information. Parents and alumni will need to request access before they are given permission to login.
