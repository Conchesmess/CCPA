New Student 🔗

Use this link to create a new student. It is a limited data set but will let you add a student quickly. At the next import more data will be added.

Ad Hoc Groups 🔗

Create groups of students for communications. You can send them each the same txt msg. You can mass email them and.or their parents.

All Txt Msgs 🔗

Get all the text messages that have been sent or received. By default it will give you the last 7 days. You can change the url to get any number of days. ie /msgs/14 will give you 14 days.

Txt Msg Replies 🔗

Get all the replies to recent text messages. By default it will give you the last 7 days. You can change the url to get any number of days. ie /msgs/14 will give you 14 days.

Undelivered Msgs 🔗

List any messages that were sent but did not get delivered.

Get Parents 🔗

Paste a comma seperated list of student emails to retrieve a list of all of the parents emails.

Borrowed Computers 🔗

List of students who have borrowed a computer.

Computer Science Mail Merge 🔗

list of names and email addresses for all students and their parents in Computer Science comma seperated by row for pasting in to a spreadsheet to do a mail merge.
